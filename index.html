Experiences

Learn more about my past and current volunteer experiences!

UI/UX Lead Designer
@ Coding 4 Community

As a Lead Designer at C4C, I work to design UI/UX projects (app and web) through visual communication, researching, wireframing, and prototyping using Figma. I collaborate along with the web developers to walk them through the process of the prototype and to ensure the interface of the application is user-friendly. Additionally, I help guide and provide feedback on projects completed by the design team members to ensure the design is ready to be handed over to the web developers working on the project.

Co-Director
@ Hacking 4 Community

One of my greatest volunteer experiences was being a co-director at C4C’s hackathon(aka Hacking 4 Community)! I managed the marketing, operations, outreach, and logistics of the event along with the rest of my team. This event definitely enhanced my leadership skills since I got to network with amazing industry leaders, make great connections, and lead events such as workshops, panels, ceremonies, and icebreakers. Additionally, I took this opportunity to host a workshop on introduction to Python, which was a success! Overall, this experience was amazing and I would love to work with my team again to host this event in 2022!

Technology Head Executive
@ Mayfield STEMinist Club

As the Technology Head Executive at Mayfield’s STEMinist Club, I worked with the technology team to develop group discussions, bring in panels, establish meeting content, and create posts for our social media presence. We held bi-weekly meetings on the fundamental basics of technology and coding to help give steminists an insight into how technology is used throughout all STEM fields, how it has its pros and cons, and its contribution to our world. I specifically discussed the A.I aspect of technology, its applications, and ethics since it forms the basis for all computer learning and is the future of all complex decision making.
